A Glimpse Into History

Cigarette cards were the world's first collectible cards, predating baseball cards and trading cards. Between the 1870s and 1940s, tobacco companies included these beautiful cards in cigarette packages as both package stiffeners and marketing tools.

Each card featured stunning illustrations and educational content on subjects ranging from sports heroes and wildlife to historical figures and technological innovations of the era.
