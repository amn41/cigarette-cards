Historical Miniature Art

Cigarette cards were the world's first collectible cards, predating modern trading cards. Between the 1870s and 1940s, tobacco companies included these beautifully illustrated cards in cigarette packages.

Each card featured detailed artwork and educational content spanning sports figures, wildlife, historical events, and the technological innovations of the era.
